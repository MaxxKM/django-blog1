{{ about.title }}About Max

Max, a 24-year-old Irish native, was born in Dublin and raised in a small village in Wicklow, where he developed a deep appreciation for the natural beauty and close-knit community of rural Ireland. After completing his secondary education, Max pursued his passion for storytelling and visual arts by studying Film Production. He spent the first three years honing his craft at Bray Institute of Further Education, followed by his final year at Coláiste Dhúlaigh, where he earned his Bachelor's Degree in Film & TV Production. Currently, Max is expanding his skill set by studying Full Stack Programming at Code Institute, with ambitions to build a successful career in the programming industry, blending his creative background with technical expertise.
